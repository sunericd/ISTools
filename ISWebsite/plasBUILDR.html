Plasmid BUILDR

The Plasmid BUILDR, or Benchmark Utility for Integrating Long DNA Reads, generates a protocol to build a desired plasmid given the sequences of the recepient plasmid and any insert genes or markers.
Several features are under development, including different file formats upload, protocol download, changing protocols to match various manufacturer's specifications, and more.
