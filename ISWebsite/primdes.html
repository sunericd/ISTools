Primer Designer

Primer designer is a primer search tool that identifies existing primers in a given sequence. Forward and reverse primers are identified with user-specified primer length, GC content, and melting temperature ranges. Primer pairs for PCR can also be identified by specifying product length range or start/stop indices.
